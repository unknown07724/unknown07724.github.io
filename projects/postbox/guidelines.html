Postbox Community Guidelines

To keep Postbox a welcoming and enjoyable space for everyone, please follow these rules:

  1. No NSFW Content
    Explicit content, adult material, or anything inappropriate is strictly prohibited. Keep it family-friendly as we do not wanna turn into reddit.
  2. No Scams or Fraud
    Do not engage in or promote any form of scamming, phishing, or fraudulent activity. Be honest and respectful in your interactions.
  3. No Hate Speech or Harassment
    Discrimination, bullying, or any form of hate speech against individuals or groups based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or any other identity is not tolerated. Respect all users.
  4. Respect Others' Privacy
    Do not share personal information of others without consent. This includes addresses, phone numbers, or private communications.
  5. Follow the Law
    Ensure that your posts do not promote illegal activities. This includes piracy, hacking, or anything that violates local or international laws.
  6. Stay On-Topic
    Keep discussions relevant to the community and subreddit youâ€™re posting in. Off-topic content can be flagged and removed.
  7. Avoid Spam
    Refrain from spamming or posting repetitive, irrelevant, or low-effort content.
  8. Be Respectful
    Treat everyone with kindness. If you disagree with someone, be respectful and express your views civilly.
  9. No Plagiarism
    Only post content that you have created or have permission to share. Always credit creators when applicable.

These rules aim to create a safe, respectful, and productive space for all users. Violating these rules may result in content removal or account suspension.
