Postbox Community Guidelines

To keep Postbox a welcoming and enjoyable space for everyone, please follow these rules:

  1. No NSFW Content
    Keep it PG, bruh. We ain't turning into Reddit’s weird corners.
  2. No Scams or Fraud
    Don’t catfish people’s wallets. No crypto “get rich quick” scams either.
  3. No Hate Speech or Harassment
    Don’t be racist, sexist, homophobic, or any -ist.
    Slurs Rule: You can say the word if you’re talking about the word (history, language, memes about why it’s cursed). You CANNOT just yeet it at someone mid-argument. That’s an insta-L.
  4. Respect Others' Privacy
    No doxxing, no leaking DMs, no FBI open-up moments.
  5. Follow the Law
    Don’t be posting pirated Minecraft accounts or hacking tutorials. Jail is not a vibe.
  6. Stay On-Topic
    Postbox ain’t your diary. Keep it relevant or it’s going in the trash.
  7. Avoid Spam
    Don’t copy-paste “HELLO” 500 times. Low-effort junk = auto cringe.
  8. Be Respectful
    Argue like a human, not like a YouTube comment section.
  9. No Plagiarism
    Don’t steal memes or art. Credit the homies or get bonked.

These rules aim to create a safe, respectful, and productive space for all users. Violating these rules may result in content removal or account suspension.
